What does each confidence level actually mean?
  • Replied — Definitive. A reply must be written by a human; no sync, proxy, or prefetch can produce one.
  • Read (open + click, or a reply) — Very high. Two independent signals, plus a proven minimum time-open.
  • Likely read — High. At least one verified open — meaning it passed every automated-fetch check (timing window, proxy/browser signature, ASN, burst pattern) and was rendered by a real client. Not proof every word was read, but a genuine human-triggered render, not a notification preview.
  • Not verifiable — Honest abstention. Apple Mail Privacy Protection, a scanner, or a prefetch was detected — we decline to claim a read rather than guess.

Classifier accuracy against our labeled test scenarios is measured, not asserted — see the project's ACCURACY.md. Real-world field accuracy across live email clients is a separate, in-progress measurement.
